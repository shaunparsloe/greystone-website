Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How far is Greystone Guesthouse from Supertubes beach?

Greystone Guesthouse is just 260 metres (about a 3-minute walk) from the beach and 300 metres from Supertubes, one of the world's most famous surf breaks. We're located in the Wavecrest area of Jeffreys Bay, with restaurants and shops within 100 metres.

What accommodation options are available?

We offer 8 en-suite rooms across three tiers: Deluxe Sea View rooms (Rooms 1–4) with balconies and sea views from ZAR 1,275/night, Standard Queen rooms (Rooms 5–6) from ZAR 1,105/night, and Budget Annex rooms (Rooms 7–8) with private entrances and kitchenettes from ZAR 1,105/night. Single occupancy rates start from ZAR 638/night.

Is breakfast included at Greystone Guesthouse?

A full cooked South African breakfast is available to order each morning — eggs, bacon, sausages, toast, fresh coffee and tea. Enjoy it in the dining room or on the balcony with sea views.

Do you have free parking and WiFi?

Yes — Greystone Guesthouse offers free private on-site parking (no reservation needed) and free WiFi throughout the property, including in all guest rooms.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 14:00 to 20:00 (please let us know your arrival time in advance). Check-out is between 07:00 and 10:00.

What activities are available near Jeffreys Bay?

Jeffreys Bay offers world-class surfing at Supertubes (300m away), beach horse riding, skydiving over the Indian Ocean, ADDO Elephant Park day trips, lion sanctuary visits, snorkelling, diving, fishing, windsurfing, golf, and hiking. Restaurants and a supermarket are within 100 metres of the guesthouse.

How do I get to Greystone from the airport?

We're 64 km (about 1 hour's drive) from Chief Dawid Stuurman International Airport (formerly Port Elizabeth Airport) in Gqeberha. A shuttle service is available at an additional charge — contact us via WhatsApp to arrange.
